# RegistryOps Phase 1 Scope

RegistryOps phase 1 activates a reduced normative core centered on four public
surfaces:

- `oci_manifest`
- `npm_package_metadata`
- `pypi_release_metadata`
- `ietf_internet_draft`

These four surfaces are the only active `surface_type` values admissible in the
phase 1 canonical event model.

Other surfaces previously identified by the project may remain documented as
strategic extensions, but they are not part of the stabilized normative core of
phase 1 until their rules are explicitly closed.

## Phase 1 Promise

RegistryOps phase 1 attests admissible public observations performed according
to a stable method. It proves what RegistryOps observed publicly and when it
observed it. It does not claim to model the full ontology of the world at every
instant.

## Phase 1 Completion Rule

Phase 1 is considered complete only when:

- the four active surfaces are fully specified
- the canonical event model is stabilized
- reobservation rules are executable and enforced
- the public contract is fixed and published
- the proof chain from journal to OTS/Bitcoin is publicly verifiable
- the migration between pre-normative history and the canonical phase 1 line is
  explicit
- a first canonical snapshot has been published as `snapshots/phase1-000001/`
